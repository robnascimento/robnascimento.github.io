About Me

I am a trained Researcher in Mathematics turned into Quantitative Finance and Data Science. I earned a PhD in Mathematics from the Université Libre de Bruxelles, working on Nonlinear Analysis and PDEs. Previously, I obtained a BSc and a MSc in Mathematics from the Universidade de Brasília, where I did work on Critical Point Theory.
