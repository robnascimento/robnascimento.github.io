Opening a Restaurant in Brussels

Brussels is a multicultural and multilingual city in the heart of Europe where more than one-third of the population is foreign. In 2018, Brussels gathered 184 different nationalities (source: Statbel). Dining in Brussels is always a delight and the city offers an extensive variety of cuisines for all foods. In this project, we investigate the key to be successful with a new restaurant opening in the city.

  • Explored datasets about demographics, location, target market, and competition
  • Used FourSquare API database for venues in Brussels
  • Employed clustering analysis to group neighborhoods and studied these clusters
